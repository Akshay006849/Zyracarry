Our Story

Crafted for Her

ZYRA CARRY was born from a simple truth: women deserve bags that are as capable, beautiful, and purposeful as they are. Every tote we create is a love letter to the modern woman.

Our Mission

Luxury That Gives Back

We partner with artisan communities across India — women weavers and craft families who have honed their skills over generations. Every ZYRA CARRY purchase supports fair wages, safe working conditions, and the preservation of India's textile heritage.

Our Values

Sustainability Is Not Optional

From natural dyes to compostable packaging, every decision we make is guided by responsibility. We design totes that last years, not seasons — because the most sustainable bag is the one you keep forever.

Our Promise

A Bag Worth Keeping

We promise you a tote that gets better with time. Stronger with use. More beautiful as it carries your stories. If for any reason you're not satisfied, we'll make it right — no questions asked within 3 days of delivery.
